NHS Low Income Scheme

If you have a low income, you may be able to get help with NHS costs through the NHS Low Income Scheme (LIS).

Universal Credit

On November 1 2015, the arrangements about who's entitled to help with health costs changed. If you receive Universal Credit, make sure you're aware of the new arrangements as you may no longer be entitled to support.
