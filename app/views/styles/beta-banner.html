Our hypothesis

We believe

  • the context of the service is what sets expectations
  • users don't understand the word or concept of alpha / beta
  • alpha / beta de-values the quality of content in a health setting (where emotions run high)
  • Users may experience banner blindness and not notice an alpha beta message, especially with cookie banners etc.
  • Users need to know upfront (before reading the content)
