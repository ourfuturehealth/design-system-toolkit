User need

As a user I need to identify and understand the important content on the page (even if I don't read all the content) so that I get the best outcome for my situation and don't endanger my health.

We believe that users scan headings. Adding a heading will make users stop and take notice of the callout. Also, changing the background colour to yellow will make it stand out from the surrounding white boxes.

Usage

The warning callout can be used to display information that is:

  • time critical
  • may have a significant health impact
  • addresses a common and/or significant misconception or patient mistake
