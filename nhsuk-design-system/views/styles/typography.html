Spacing

We have used single-direction margin declarations to easily define vertical rhythm (external link).

We currently use pixels (px) for margin spacing however these will be converted to ems after testing.

Spacing rules

  • spacing is multiples of 8px
  • line heights are multiples of 4px

Headings

H1 (nhsuk-heading-xl) sizes:

  • Desktop: 48 / 56 - margin 72px
  • Mobile: 32 / 36 - margin 56px

You can use the utility class nhsuk-heading-xl to get the sizing and spacing of a h1 on non h1 elements.

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

<h1>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</h1>

<span class="nhsuk-heading-xl">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</span>
