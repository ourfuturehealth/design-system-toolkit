La Bomba dance exercise workout

2 minutes 30 seconds

Shake it up Latino style as this dance fitness class gives you a fun, upbeat and vigorous workout but be prepared to sweat!

Media review dates

Media last reviewed: 1 Jun 2016

Media review due: 10 Mar 2019

Instructor Chloe takes you through a combination of dance moves set to Latin American dance music, hip hop, Afrobeats and R&B. Hopefully you'll be having so much fun you won't even notice how hard you're working.

The class runs for 45 minutes and is suitable for all levels. You'll need trainers and some water, and you may want to have a small towel to hand.

These video fitness workouts have been created by InstructorLive and range from 10 minutes to 45 minutes. Please note that these videos are recorded sessions of previously live webcasts. 

If you liked this class, you may enjoy Belly dancing for beginners or Aerobics for beginners.
